Cat Info

Cats were once domesticated long ago by humans from wild cats like wolves and dogs. Over many centuries cats have turned into the pretty docile housepet you see today. Once worshipped by Egyptians, cats are now widely celebrated on the Internet by their funny antics and appearences. This may have been cause for cats having such large egos about themselves. Did you know that cats think humans are crappily made cats? They believe we cannnot properly hunt or function as cats and thus place themselves above us in their minds. Dogs however just love us unconditionally while cats tolerate out ineptitude.

Cats' popularity on the net has been around for a long time. What made them so popular in the first place is like asking why certain videos go viral. We don't know how or why, they just are. Cats are also a popular house pet because of their low maintenance compared to other animals. Once you get past the hefty vet bills for vaccinations and such, cats really only need food and water. If the cat goes outside enough, it may not even need a litterbox. Cats sleep for 18hrs of the day so you won't have to worry about leaving them at the house while you work unlike a dog.
